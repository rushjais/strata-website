Operations FAQ

How does the pilot integration work? expand_more

The founder and our lead engineers work directly with your team to map your technical documentation architecture and ingest your proprietary manuals into your dedicated RAG pipeline within 48 hours.

Is my data secure? expand_more

Yes, Strata uses isolated vectors for every client. Your technical manuals and operational data are never used to train base models and remain entirely encrypted within your organization's private environment.

What hardware is required? expand_more

Strata is platform-agnostic and designed for rugged environments. It runs on existing shop tablets (iPad/Android), field laptops, or integrated AR headsets like HoloLens 2 and RealWear.

What happens after the 90-day pilot? expand_more

At the end of the 90 days, you'll receive a performance report demonstrating ROI. You can then choose to transition to a permanent per-shop license at a discounted "Founder Partner" rate.
